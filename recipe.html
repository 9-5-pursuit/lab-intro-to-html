The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks good when made right. I am not exactly a fan of peanut butter and jelly myself but whenever I do make it, I never receive any complaints. In fact everyone loves my PBJ.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Enjoy with a glass of cold almond milk.
