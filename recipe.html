The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is one of my favorite sandwiches. This receipe provides the perfect balance of ingredients and looks amazing when made correctly.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of cinnamon raisin bread
  • 1/4 cup of smooth Jiffy Peanut Butter
  • 2 table spoons of Bonne Maman Wild Blueberry Preserves

Directions

  1. Toast the bread to your desired level of darkness.
  2. Spread the peanut butter evenly across 1 slice of bread.
  3. Spread the preserves evenly across the other slice.
  4. Sandwich the slices together. Cut the sandwich diagonally or horizontally into equal parts.
