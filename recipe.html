The perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwish. it has the perfect blance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of jelly

Directions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly on the inside.
  4. Enjoy!
