Peanut-Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Made out of peanut butter and fruit jelly of choise spread on bread

Ingredients

  • 2 slices od white bread
  • 1 jar of preffered jelly
  • 1 jar of preffered peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting borad.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly on one of the slices of bread
  3. On the other slice, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter
  4. Gently place the slices toget to form a sandwith.
  5. With the sharp knife, carfully cut the sandwich in half
  6. Serve with glass of milk on the side
