A Monster PB&J

Description

This is a monster of a PB&J! Don't eat this before bedtime kiddos!!

Ingredients

  • Revenge
  • Guts
  • Bugs
  • Stuff
  • Peanuts
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Monster Drool

Directions

  1. Gather all of your ingrediants
    • The ingredients must be gathered no more than one week before a blorange moon
  2. Make a regular PB&J
  3. Summon a monster
  4. Feed the monster the PB&J and get them to exact your revenge upon your enemies.
If this is the wrong type of Monster PB&J, and you do not wish to exact your vengeance upon your enemies, try this normal monster PB&J Sandiwch.
