The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich ia my favorite sandwich. it has th perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side of the bread.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on the top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Heat it up for a 30 seconds.
  7. Enjoy!
