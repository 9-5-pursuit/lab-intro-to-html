The Perfect PB&J

Description

The O.G. sandwich, Peanut Butter and Jelly. One of the simplest meals to make that will never go out of style.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoon of jelly

Directions

  1. On one slice of bread, spread peanut butter evenly over the bread.
  2. On one slice of bread, spread jelly evenly over the bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in
  4. Cut diagonally
  5. Serve and enjoy
