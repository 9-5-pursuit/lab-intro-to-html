The Perfect PB & J

Description

A peanut butter sandwich is the tastest sandwich that every child in their childhood like to.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A knife
  • A butter knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. Use a butter knife to spread a layer of peanut butter on one of the slice of bread.
  3. On the other bread, spread a layer of grape jelly.
  4. Carefully place two spreaded of the slices of bread together.
  5. Use a sharpe knife to cute teh sandwich in half.
