The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a staple sandwich in the United States; it's a healthy sandwich, packed with protein, and an easy meal to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 teaspoons of grape jelly
  • 1 teaspoon of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspons of butter

Directions

  1. Heat skillet to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on other side of buttered bread. Spread peanut butter on other slice (unbuttered side).
  3. Place bread slices together sp peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  4. Cook on preheated skillet until golden brown and heated thoroughly (4 minutes a side).
  5. Enjoy!
