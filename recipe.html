The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favourite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of white bread
  • 1 Jar of grape jelly
  • 1 Jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Place both slices of bread on cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife spread even layer of peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Clean the knife and spread an even layer of jelly on the other slice of bread.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together so the peanut butter and jelly make contact.
  5. Using the sharp knife cut the sandwich in half.
  6. (Optional) Using the sharp knife cut off the crust off the sandwich.
