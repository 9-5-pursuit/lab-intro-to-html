The Perfect PB&j

Description

This peanut better and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has perfect balance of ingerdients and looks great when made

Ingredients

  • 2 lices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both sied of berad next to each otheron a cutting board.
  2. with the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the other side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place two halves together so that the jelly is on the top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwitch in half.
